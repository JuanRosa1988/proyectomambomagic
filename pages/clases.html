Grilla de Clases Semanales

Mambo Absolute Begginer

Ideal for those new to dance, this course covers fundamental movements and basic techniques. Students need to complete two months of this course to move onto Level 1.

Mambo Nivel 1

A step up from Beginner 1, this progressive course builds on basic skills and introduces more complex techniques. Now, it's highly recommended that you start attending the optional additional classes for faster progress. Students must complete three months of Level 1 to move onto Level 2.

Mambo Nivel 2

A step up from Beginner 1, this progressive course builds on basic skills and introduces more complex techniques. Now, it's highly recommended that you start attending the optional additional classes for faster progress. Students must complete three months of Level 2 to move onto Level 3.

Mambo Nivel 3

For seasoned dancers, this track provides challenging routines and advanced techniques to push your skills to the next level. To progress in body movement, styling, precision, & grounded movements we highly recommend taking advantage of our additional classes!
